How to Play

Ladders:

If your game piece lands on the base of a ladder, move it to the square where the ladder ends. Climb the ladder to progress quickly!

Snakes:

If your game piece lands on the head of a snake, move it to the square where the snake's tail is located. Beware of the snakes; they can set you back!

Extra Roll:

If you roll a 6 on the dice, you get an extra roll. Take advantage of this to reach the final square faster.

Winning:

The first player to land exactly on the final square wins the game!
